NEW LAW — “All Sport Graphics Live in the Footer”

Any situational graphic (MLB diamond, balls/strikes/outs pips, NFL downs, NBA foul pips, soccer cards, etc.) must render in the footer row, right next to your chips (inning/period, clock, wind).

The header stays text-only; the body stays the short “why” message.

Tiny polish (baseball-specific but universal-friendly)

Diamond & pips are small (6–8px) so they sit neatly with chips.

Keep wind as a chip; it’s textual but useful across sports.

If batter/on-deck matters, leave that in the people pills above the footer (faster eye-scan), while the situational graphics live in the footer.

NBA/NFL/etc.: footer shows their own graphic pips (when present) + chips (period/clock, red zone, etc.).
