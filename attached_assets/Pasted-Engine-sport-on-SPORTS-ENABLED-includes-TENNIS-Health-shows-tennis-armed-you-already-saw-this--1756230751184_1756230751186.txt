Tuning tips (AI-only)

Too quiet? lower opp_min_priority → 86–88, shorten cooldown to 60–75s.

Too chatty? raise opp_min_priority → 92, lengthen cooldown to 150–180s.

Keep opportunities pre-moment only (your shouldProbeAI gate): late-set 30–30/receiver-favored points and late tiebreaks.
