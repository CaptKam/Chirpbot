Quick notes from the audit (so you’re oriented)

Dedup is solid: you’ve got a global deduper (server/services/dedup.ts) using a situation fingerprint (gamePk, inning/half, outs, runners, batterId, wind bucket, etc.). I left it intact and just made the base-engine’s refire guard configurable to avoid double‑gating.

MLB live feed: you’re already probing multiple paths (liveData.plays.currentPlay, linescore.offense, and fallbacks) to find the current batter—good coverage.

Weather: getWeatherData is present but returns null without a real ACCUWEATHER_API_KEY. That’s fine; alerts still work—just won’t show wind details until you add a key.

Telegram: implemented; stays off unless you set TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN/TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID.
